About Gadi

Session Leader, Choreographer & Event Producer

Through the framework of his vocation, Gadi works tirelessly to promote, expand, and stimulate interest in Israeli folk dance, bringing national and international recognition to the art form — drawing in younger generations while continuing to respect and provide a place for established veterans and the heritage of Israeli dance.

Artistic Director, Karmiel Dance Festival Chair, Hebrew Song Division, Ministry of Culture Member, Israel Council for Culture and Art Guinness World Record holder in folk dance
